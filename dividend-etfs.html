Dividend ETFs

Focus on income generation. Dividend ETFs invest in companies that pay regular dividends, offering investors a steady income stream.

These ETFs are suitable for those seeking passive income while still participating in the equity markets.

Ticker Name Details
VYM Vanguard High Dividend Yield ETF Tracks the FTSE High Dividend Yield Index, focusing on companies with higher-than-average dividend yields.
DVY iShares Select Dividend ETF Seeks to track the Dow Jones U.S. Select Dividend Index, focusing on high-dividend-yielding U.S. companies.
SDY SPDR S&P Dividend ETF Tracks the S&P High Yield Dividend Aristocrats Index, focusing on companies that have consistently increased dividends for at least 20 years.
SCHD Schwab U.S. Dividend Equity ETF Tracks the Dow Jones U.S. Dividend 100 Index, focusing on high-quality U.S. companies with a history of consistent dividends.
DGRO iShares Core Dividend Growth ETF Seeks to track the Morningstar US Dividend Growth Index, focusing on companies with strong dividend growth potential.
